[ WHO I AM ]

I'm Vaishnav N, an enthusiastic and driven aspiring Data Scientist from Kannur, Kerala, currently pursuing a specialized Diploma in Data Science. I thrive on transforming raw, messy data into clean, actionable insights that power real-world decisions.
